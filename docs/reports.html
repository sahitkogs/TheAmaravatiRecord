Methodology & Sources

Every report published here is built on a public data source. The primary source for the land-pooling investigations is the APCRDA Land Pooling Scheme portal at gis.apcrda.org/lps. Surname classification uses a hand-curated corpus drawn from nineteen independent community sources, augmented with per-name classification via Google Gemini 2.5 Flash. Cross-check rates and error bars are reported in the dashboard methodology tab.

Personally identifying information — farmer given names and plot identifiers — is masked server-side before publication. Aggregate community and village statistics are unaffected by the masking.
